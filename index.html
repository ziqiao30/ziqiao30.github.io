Featured Research

Surface-Based Manipulation with Modular Foldable Robots

Using flat surfaces as minimalist end-effectors to translate, rotate, and flip objects via closed-loop control. Published in npj Robotics, 2026.

01

Controlling the Legs of a 12t Walking Excavator Using Reinforcement Learning

A reinforcement learning locomotion controller for a 12-ton walking excavator. Trained on mixed terrain in simulation with PPO, validated on real hardware.

Master Thesis

Learning to Land on Flexible Structures

RL-trained drone that lands on and maintains contact with flexible tree branches. PPO/SAC with domain randomization.

02

Neural Network Based Battery Management System for Electric Vehicles

A BMS based on LSTM neural networks to predict battery SOC from real-time voltage, current, and temperature.

03

A Multi-Agent Robot System (ABU Robocon, 2nd Prize)

Multi-sensor fusion positioning and motion planning for two collaborative robots. National 2nd prize.

04

The Frisbee Robot with Machine Vision (ABU Robocon, 3rd + Best Technical)

A frisbee-throwing robot with vision-based targeting. Six design iterations, 3rd place and best technical award.

05

Model Predictive Control for Asynchronous Motor Velocity Control

Bachelor thesis: cascaded MPC controllers with a sensorless speed observer for asynchronous motors.

06

A Quadrotor Aircraft for Object Tracking
