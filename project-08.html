Project 08

Paper Quantity Detection System Based on Capacitance Variation (National 1st Prize)

Designed a paper quantity measurement device based on LC parallel resonance for the 2019 National Undergraduate Electronics Design Contest. Won national first prize.

The Sensor

Custom circular PCB — LC parallel resonator for capacitance-based paper count measurement

Technical Details

  • Designed a sensor consisting of an LC parallel resonator with an inductance and two copper plates
  • Created a piecewise nonlinear mathematical model relating paper count to resonance frequency
  • Fabricated and developed the equipment, capable of measuring up to 80 sheets of paper

National 1st Prize — 2019 National Undergraduate Electronics Design Contest, August 2019
