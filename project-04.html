Project 04

The Frisbee Robot with a Machine Vision System (ABU Robocon, 3rd Place + Best Technical Award)

Designed a frisbee-throwing robot for the 16th ABU Robocon. After six design iterations, the robot won third place and the best technical award at the national competition.

The Robot & Arena

The frisbee robot — camera for vision, three launch mechanisms with adjustable pitch and yaw, frisbee cartridges
Competition arena — the frisbee must pass through the ring and land in the target disk

Competition Video

16th ABU Asia-Pacific Robot Contest in China, July 2017

Technical Details

  • Designed a frisbee-throwing mechanism with machine vision-based targeting
  • Three sets of launch mechanisms with adjustable pitch and yaw
  • Integrated real-time computer vision (camera) for target detection and tracking
  • Iterated through six design versions to optimize throwing accuracy and reliability

3rd Place + Best Technical Award — 16th ABU Asia-Pacific Robot Contest in China, July 2017
